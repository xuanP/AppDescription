Image Compressor is a simple, efficient photo compression tool that helps you:

  • Compress up to 16 photos at once

  • Adjust compression quality from 1% to 100%
  • 100% offline processing - your photos never leave your device
  • No ads, no in-app purchases, completely free

ğŸ†˜ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I compress photos?

A: It's simple!

  1. Tap "Select Image(s)" button
  2. Choose 1-16 photos from your library
  3. Drag the slider to adjust compression quality (default: 50%)
  4. Swipe the card to compare Before/After
  5. Tap "Save to Album" to save compressed photos

Q: Are my photos uploaded to a server?

A: No! All processing happens 100% on your device. Your photos never leave your phone. The app works completely offline and collects zero data.

Q: What's the difference between single and batch mode?

A:

  • Single Mode: Select 1 photo for quick compression with instant preview
  • Batch Mode: Select 2-16 photos to compress them all at once, then choose which ones to save

Q: Why is the app asking for photo library access?

A: We need access to:

  • Let you select photos for compression
  • Save compressed photos back to your library

We never access your photos without your permission, and we never upload them anywhere.

Q: Which compression quality should I choose?

A: It depends on your needs:

  • 90% (High Quality): Nearly lossless, 40-60% smaller
  • 50% (Default): Great balance, 70-80% smaller
  • 15% (Maximum): Heavy compression, 90-95% smaller

Q: The app isn't working properly

A: Try these steps:

  1. Make sure you're running iOS 15.0 or later
  2. Try closing and reopening the app
  3. Restart your iPhone
  4. If the problem persists, contact us (see below)
